## Overview

My research focuses on **igneous petrology and volcanic geochemistry** of **young volcanic systems**, with emphasis on **ocean-island volcanism** and **explosive silicic volcanism**. I integrate stratigraphy, petrology, geochemistry, and isotopes to reconstruct eruptive histories and magmatic processes.
